Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Auto Subtitler

What video formats are supported?
Auto Subtitler supports all major video formats including MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV, WebM, WMV, and more. If you have a video format that isn't working, please contact us.
Do I need an internet connection to use Auto Subtitler?
An internet connection is required for the initial setup and for translation features. Once the models are downloaded, basic transcription can work offline.
What are the system requirements?
Auto Subtitler requires Windows 10 or later (64-bit), 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended), and an NVIDIA GPU with CUDA support for GPU acceleration (optional but recommended).
Is there a free trial available?
Yes! Auto Subtitler is completely free and open source. You can download and use it without any limitations.
How accurate is the transcription?
Using OpenAI's Whisper model, Auto Subtitler achieves up to 99% accuracy on clear audio. Accuracy may vary with background noise, accents, or technical terminology.
